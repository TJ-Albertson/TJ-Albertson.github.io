Servers

These are some retired servers from my work, HP and SuperMicro, that I use as a lab and for personal storage/hosting. I am really interested in compute clusters so I have used these to experiment with all kinds of different software like Kubernetes, Proxmox, CephFS, and Vmware Vsan.

Even though I am not a big fan of it, I use Vmware Vsan as the backing virtualization platform because we use Vmware products at my work.

For my personal stuff I host video game servers and store videos with data redundancy and cloud backups.
